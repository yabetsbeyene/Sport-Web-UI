Who We Are

More Than a Gym

APEX was founded on the belief that fitness is a team sport. We combine world-class programming with a culture that holds every member accountable and lifts every member up — from first-time athletes to seasoned competitors.

Our 1,800 m² facility houses dedicated zones for strength, conditioning, recovery, and group training — all staffed by certified coaches who are passionate about your progress, not just your attendance.
